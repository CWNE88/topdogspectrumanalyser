Top Dog Spectrum Analyser — Menu Tree
======================================
[ ] = soft button submenu  |  * = dynamic (source-dependent)

HARDWARE BUTTONS
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

[FREQUENCY]
  ├── Centre Frequency
  ├── Start Frequency
  ├── Stop Frequency
  ├── Centre Freq ÷ 2
  └── Centre Freq × 2

[SPAN]  * (contents depend on active source)
  ├── (Sweep source)
  │     ├── Span
  │     └── Full Span
  ├── (RTL Samples)
  │     ├── 250 kHz | 1.024 MHz | 1.44 MHz | 1.8 MHz
  │     │   2.0 MHz | 2.048 MHz | 2.4 MHz
  │     └── Zero Span ──► Free Run | Rise | Fall | Trigger Level
  ├── (HackRF Samples)
  │     ├── 2 MHz | 4 MHz | 8 MHz | 10 MHz | 16 MHz | 20 MHz
  │     └── Zero Span ──► Free Run | Rise | Fall | Trigger Level
  ├── (Microphone Samples)
  │     ├── 8 kHz | 11.025 kHz | 16 kHz | 22.05 kHz
  │     │   44.1 kHz | 48 kHz | 96 kHz
  │     └── Zero Span ──► Free Run | Rise | Fall | Trigger Level
  └── (No source / Sweep)
        └── Span

[AMPLITUDE]
  ├── Ref Level
  ├── Scale
  │     ├── Log
  │     └── Linear
  ├── Log dB/div
  │     ├── 1 dB/div
  │     ├── 2 dB/div
  │     ├── 5 dB/div
  │     ├── 10 dB/div
  │     └── 20 dB/div
  └── Trace Normalise

[INPUT]  (key: I)
  ├── RTL-SDR
  │     ├── Samples
  │     └── Sweep
  ├── HackRF
  │     ├── Samples
  │     └── Sweep
  └── Microphone         (starts immediately; channel via Analysis → FFT → Channel)

[ANALYSIS]  (key: N)
  ├── FFT
  │     ├── Window
  │     │     ├── Hamming
  │     │     ├── Hanning
  │     │     └── Rectangle
  │     ├── Sample Size
  │     │     ├── 512
  │     │     ├── 1024
  │     │     ├── 2048
  │     │     └── 4096
  │     └── Channel           (Microphone only)
  │           ├── Mono
  │           ├── Left
  │           ├── Right
  │           └── Stereo
  ├── PSD (dB/Hz)
  ├── Constellation
  │     ├── Density
  │     └── Scatter
  └── Duty Cycle

[MARKER]
  ├── Mkr 1 Freq
  ├── Mkr 2 Freq
  ├── Mkr 1 Power
  ├── Mkr 2 Power
  ├── Mkr → Peak
  ├── Next Peak
  ├── Mkr → Centre
  └── Clear All

[TRACE]  (key: T)
  ├── Live On/Off
  ├── Averaging
  │     ├── Average Off
  │     ├── Exp ×2
  │     ├── Exp ×4
  │     ├── Exp ×8
  │     ├── Exp ×16
  │     ├── Lin ×4
  │     ├── Lin ×16
  │     └── Lin ×64
  ├── Hold
  │     ├── Max Hold
  │     ├── Min Hold
  │     └── Clear Hold
  ├── Persistence
  │     ├── Off
  │     ├── Short
  │     ├── Medium
  │     └── Long
  └── Memory
        ├── Store A
        ├── Show A
        ├── Store B
        ├── Show B
        ├── A − B
        └── Clear All

[DISPLAY]  (key: D)
  ├── Display Line
  │     ├── On / Off
  │     └── Level
  ├── Pk Threshold
  ├── Excursion
  └── Log Freq

[PRESET]
  ├── Recall  ──► Slot 1 … Slot 8   (shows saved name or "Empty")
  ├── Save    ──► Slot 1 … Slot 8
  ├── Name    ──► Slot 1 … Slot 8   (then type name on keypad)
  └── Delete  ──► Slot 1 … Slot 8

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
DISPLAY VIEW BUTTONS  (soft menu appears automatically on press)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

[2D]        → soft menu: (blank — no options)
[3D]        → soft menu: History Lines | Grid On/Off | Auto Rotate
                           └── History Lines ──► 50 | 100 | 200 | 300 | 500 lines
[WATERFALL] → soft menu: (blank — no options)
[SURFACE]   → soft menu: History Lines | Auto Rotate
                           └── History Lines ──► 10 | 25 | 50 | 100 | 200 lines

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
KEYPAD UNIT BUTTONS  (labels change with entry mode)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  Frequency / Span entry : GHz | MHz | kHz | Hz
  Ref Level / Threshold  :  (blank) | (blank) | (blank) | dBm
  Excursion              :  (blank) | (blank) | (blank) | dB
  Preset Name            :  (blank) | (blank) | (blank) | OK

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
KEYBOARD SHORTCUTS
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  F  — Frequency menu
  S  — Span menu
  A  — Amplitude menu
  I  — Input menu
  N  — Analysis menu
  M  — Marker menu
  T  — Trace menu
  D  — Display menu
  P  — Preset menu (button only)
  Backspace — Go back one menu level
  Alt+Enter — Pop display out to separate window
