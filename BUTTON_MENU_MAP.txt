──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  FREQUENCY  [F]
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  ├── Centre Frequency          (keypad entry → sets centre freq)
  ├── Start Frequency           (keypad entry → sets start freq)
  ├── Stop Frequency            (keypad entry → sets stop freq)
  ├── Centre Freq ÷ 2           (halves current centre freq)
  └── Centre Freq × 2           (doubles current centre freq)

──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  MARKER  [K]
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  ├── Mkr 1 Freq                 (keypad entry → places marker 1 at frequency)
  ├── Mkr 2 Freq                 (keypad entry → places marker 2 at frequency)
  ├── Mkr 1 Power                (reads power at marker 1 position)
  ├── Mkr 2 Power                (reads power at marker 2 position)
  ├── Mkr → Peak                 (moves marker 1 to highest peak)
  ├── Next Peak                  (moves to next lower peak)
  ├── Mkr → Centre               (sets centre freq to marker 1 position)
  ├── Clear All                  (removes all markers)
  └── Peak List                  (shows list of detected peaks)    [paginated]

──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  PEAK SEARCH  [P]  (hard button, not a menu)
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  Toggles automatic peak tracking on the live trace.
  Incompatible with Max Hold (disabled when max hold is active).

──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  MAX HOLD / MIN HOLD  [X]  (hard button, cycles Max → Min → Off)
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  Cycles: Max Hold → Min Hold → Off
  Trace → Hold submenu provides the same control plus Clear Hold.

──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  HOLD  [Space]  (hard button)
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  Freezes the current display frame (pauses data acquisition display).

──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  VERT/HORIZ  [V]  (hard button)
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  Toggles between horizontal (display left, controls right) and vertical
  (display top, controls bottom) panel layout.

──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  Alt+Enter  (keyboard shortcut — no button)
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  Pop-out / return display widget into a separate floating window.
  OpenGL displays (3D, Surface) are cloned rather than reparented.
